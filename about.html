About Me

Get to know the photographer behind the lens

Hello, I'm Mike Ukonzile

I'm a professional photographer with over 10 years of experience capturing special moments and creating timeless memories. My passion for photography began when I received my first camera at age 12, and I've been perfecting my craft ever since.

My style is a blend of photojournalism and fine art photography, with a touch of classic elegance. I specialize in wedding, portrait, and commercial photography, with a keen eye for detail and composition.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me teaching photography workshops, exploring nature, or spending time with my family and two golden retrievers.
