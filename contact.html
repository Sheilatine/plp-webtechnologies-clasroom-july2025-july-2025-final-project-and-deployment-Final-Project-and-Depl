Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my session?

For portrait sessions, I recommend booking 2-3 weeks in advance. For weddings, it's best to book 6-12 months ahead to ensure availability. Event coverage should be booked at least 1 month in advance.

What is your cancellation policy?

I understand that plans change. For cancellations made more than 14 days before your session, you'll receive a full refund. Within 14 days, I offer to reschedule your session. Weddings and events have a specific contract with different terms.

How long until I receive my photos?

Portrait sessions are typically delivered within 2 weeks. Wedding galleries are delivered within 6-8 weeks. Rush processing is available for an additional fee.

Do you offer prints and albums?

Yes, I offer professional printing services and custom-designed albums. You can order these through your online gallery after your session.
